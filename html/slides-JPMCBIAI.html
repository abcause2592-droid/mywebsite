The Stack 03 / 05

Our environment

Three Layers,
Three Roles

Databricks

The data lakehouse. Pipelines, modeled data, cohort outputs. Genie provides in-platform natural language querying for analysts who have access.

Governed · In platform
Sigma

Structured, governed reporting. Dashboards and defined metrics for stakeholders who need consistent views of recruiting data.

Governed · Reporting layer
Claude

Natural language layer that sits across both. Pulls from Databricks or Sigma, formats outputs, and delivers them to people who don't have platform access.

Ad-hoc · Last mile

Databricks and Sigma handle what analysts see. Claude handles what everyone else receives.

Capability 04 / 05

How they work together

Genie Gets the Data.
Claude Delivers It.

Databricks Genie

In-platform natural language querying. An analyst asks a question, Genie queries the lakehouse, returns a result — all inside Databricks.

For: analysts with Databricks access
Claude / Cowork

Takes that data (or queries Databricks directly via MCP) and packages it as a formatted report, email, or summary — for anyone who doesn't touch Databricks.

For: hiring managers, recruiters, leadership

These tools aren't competing. Genie deepens what analysts can do inside the platform. Claude extends what the data can reach — to stakeholders who need the insight but not the interface.

Use Cases 05 / 05

What this looks like for us

Where It Fits in Recruiting BI

Early Careers Cohort Reports

High-volume, repeatable cohort data pulled from Databricks — formatted and delivered to program managers who don't have platform access.

High volume · Structured data · Repeatable
Ad-Hoc Pipeline Summaries

A recruiter or hiring manager asks a one-off question about their pipeline. Claude queries Sigma or Databricks and returns a plain-language answer or formatted summary.

No SQL · No Databricks access needed
Stakeholder Briefings

Weekly or event-driven recruiting snapshots — drafted, formatted, and ready to send. Pulls from live data rather than manually assembled.

Replaces manual ticket work
Cross-Tool Workflows

Claude connects Databricks, Sigma, Gmail, Slack, and Calendar in one workflow. Data pulled, formatted, and delivered — without toggling between platforms.

Multi-system · No code
Quarterly Business Reviews

Pulling updated recruiting figures ahead of QBRs for senior HR and recruiting leadership. Claude queries the latest metrics from Databricks or Sigma and assembles a clean briefing — faster than the manual process.

High visibility · Leadership-facing · Repeatable each quarter
