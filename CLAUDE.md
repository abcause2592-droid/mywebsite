## Leland Coaching — Rules Claude Must Always Follow

**Resume tailoring:** Never suggest per-role resume customization. The right approach is role-profile optimization — build the strongest version targeting a *role type*, and it travels across all companies in that lane. Per-role tailoring is a waste of client time and makes the resume feel generic to human readers.

**LinkedIn connections:** Under 500 connections is a critical issue, not a minor note. Always flag it explicitly and treat it as a primary blocker.

**Finance background:** Any client with a finance degree or finance role history — flag fintech/financial services as a targeting angle. It's an underused asset.

**Revenue:** Leland takes 23%. Andrew keeps 77% of gross.

**Client motivation:** If a partner/spouse was driving the outreach and the client seemed passive or depressed on the call, flag this as a conversion risk. A client who isn't bought in won't do the work.

**Contract endings:** If a client's last role was a contract that ended (vs. a layoff), label it correctly — it's a cleaner narrative than being laid off.
